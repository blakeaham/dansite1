Dan thinks everyone should always be giving back.

Dan likes to do good things with his time and talent. He has performed and organized multiple fundraiser shows for Toys for Tots, Luekemia and Lymphoma Society, The Mideest Battle Buddies, and more.

Podcasts are great. Dan hosted one in 2015 and '16. The MidWest Digest.

Dan hosted The MidWestDigest, a project headed by some of the best comics in Wichita, KS. They talked to musicians, comics, artists, and anyone else who would sit in front of a microphone with them. You can go back and listen to the archived episodes here.

Dan The Man captures the hilarity in everyday situations and experiences. He’s a lifetime vagabond with Boston roots.
Currently based out of the Midwest, his high energy performances will take you from the mundane to the insane.
He’s appeared in venues like The Ambassador, Comedy Parlor and Loony Bin as well millions of viral views, Dan The Man is a can’t miss act and has been called “fiercely funny” by The Wichita Eagle
